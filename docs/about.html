About Us

We started with the question, Why Lead? And this question led to a simple survey that we shared with leaders across many organizations.

The results were shocking. Leaders were struggling. Alot of organizational success hinged on their participation, yet it felt like they were the only ones who cared. We understood the work and life of a leader, and we said, it’s time to step in and help.

And that’s how WhyLead was started. To partner with leaders and organizations and provide the support needed to thrive.
